About MUCSA

The Melbourne University Computer Students Association (MUCSA) is a club for students from The University of Melbourne who have an interest in Information Technology.

Our Goals

  • Connect like-minded students through social events such as BBQs, Pizza lunches and Drinks.
  • Encourage students to further their education through technical events such as programming competitions.
  • Connect students with potential employers looking to meet high quality graduates.

Join Us

Thanks to the Student Services Administration Fee joining MUCSA is free from 2012 onwards. Membership is open to all currently enrolled students at The University of Melbourne.

To join, simply fill in your details on the form here. We will add you to our mailing list and you'll receive notice next time we're running an event.

While you're at it, join us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter.
